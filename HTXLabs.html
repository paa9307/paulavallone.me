EMPACT

HTX Labs' VR Training Simulations for Quest 3S, Quest 3, VIVE Focus 3, PC, Mobile

A VR and 2D training simulator made at HTX Labs in Unreal Engine 4/5. I scripted interactive training sequences. I worked on several procedures that have been fully released to the client, the U.S. Air Force. Sometimes worked on implementing procedures from scratch and sometimes porting from EMPACT II (UE4) to EMPACT III (UE5). Interactions needed to be replicated properly for lesson recording and playback. Regularly debugged and participated in code reviews. Collaborated with production to design interacts that satisfied the client. Communicated with artists for mesh and animation adjustments. Groomed engineering tickets in Jira. Documented walkthroughs of training procedures in Confluence.
