Game Jams

Stormlight Manor
2D retro game made in Unity for 72 hour game jam, Ludum Dare 51. In addition to coming up with the initial concept, I worked on game design, created the majority of the 15 levels, and contributed to character/enemy art. Out of 1,703 jam submissions we came in 328th place for Theme and 659th place Overall. We are currently making this into a full game.

Crapshot Saloon & Deluxe
2D Unity game made for GMTK Game Jam 2022 in 48 hours. I helped design the game and did the character art. Out of 6,168 jam submissions we came in 385th place for Creativity and 441st place Overall. We made a post jam "Deluxe Edition" that further polished the game and added new difficulties plus an endless mode.

VRn't
2D arcade game made in Unity for 72 hour game jam, Ludum Dare 41. I helped design the game and did all of the artwork and animations. Out of 2,017 jam submissions we came in 17th place for Humor and 185th place Overall.
