Hunter's Legacy: Echoes of the Past
(Project Roosevelt)

Shelved Prototype

A FPS/VR game prototype made at The Devhouse Agency in Unreal Engine 5 for a client. Programmed player mechanics and game features to work as both a normal First Person Shooter and a VR game. Made game function with mouse & keyboard, controller, and VR controllers. Worked on level design and gameplay flow. Implemented menus and closed captions. Assisted with NPC AI logic.

(Video playthrough of the prototype)















The Devhouse Agency Website
Description:

Hunter's Legacy: Echoes of the Past is a prototype for an open world narrative hunting game. It could be played in FPS with keyboard, FPS with controller, and VR. The demo had 2 paths: Linear narrative and Free roam.

Equipment:

* Hunting Rifle (Zoom, Reload, Shoot, Safety)
* Binoculars (Zoom)
* Wind Detector Spray
* GPS
* Rangefinder
